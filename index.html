About Us

iDeal Bachata School is a bachata dance school located in Stockholm with a wide range of classes for beginners as well as advanced dancers. You can also book us for company events, bachelorette/bachelor parties, workshops and private dance instruction. No matter what you are interested in, what all our offerings have in common is that we want every student to have fun and meet new people at the same time that you grow as a dancer.

Contact

If you have any questions about courses, company events, parties or anything else please send us an email at info@idealbachataschool.com, and we will respond as soon as we can.
