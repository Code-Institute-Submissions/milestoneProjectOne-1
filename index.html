What is Bachata?

Bachata is a partner dance that originated in the Dominican Republic. It is intimately connected with bachata music, which is best described as slower and more sensual than salsa.

Over the years many different forms of bachata have appeared. At iDeal Bachata we teach a blend of the styles known as Bachata Fusion, which incorporates moves from other latin american dances like Salsa and Tango, and Bachata Sensual, a more sensual version of bachata that takes inspiration from Lamba Zouk by incorporating body waves and isolations.

The most structured, detailed and easy way to learn how to dance

Our mission at iDeal Bachata School is to help you to fully get the benefits of dancing bachata in your life, and I dare say we do it better than other schools.

  • Most other school do not have a curriculum meant to bring you from complete beginner to master of the dancefloor (or to whatever level you want to go). The teachers often make up a sequence to teach you during the class that does not build on previous levels, and each teacher will teach you different things even if they are teaching the same level, meaning that you can easily miss out on learning a lot. Unlike our competitors we have a step-by-step curriculum, built around our 6-pattern turn system, that all our teachers follow, and will teach you all the moves you will need.
  • We teach moves, not sequences. Whilst other schools will teach you a sequence, often comprised of many moves you already know, and then leave it up to you to break out the different moves, we at iDeal Bachata School teach you the moves themselves. We believe this makes your dance more unique since you are the one combining the moves together, as opposed to following a set script.
  • What most teachers neglect to tell you, because they want to sell you more classes, is that on the dance floor it is not the amount of moves you know that matters. The MOST important thing is the connection you have with your partner. If you can create a good connection then all you really need is a handful of moves. We teach you the elements of creating the right type of connection right from the start. Bad for business, good for you.
  • Bachata music is not all the same. With varying intro lengths, parts of songs that contain no beat, half-counts, and so on different types of music work better with certain types of moves. Not only do we teach you how to perform the moves correctly, we also give tips and inspiration on to what types of music certain moves will work best at.

We provide you with one of the most structured, detailed and easy ways to learn how to dance bachata. Our system will turn you into a good dancer, a dancer whose strengths lie in the areas most important to giving you and your partner a magical dance experience. Beginner Courses

Beginner Level 1

This is our course for absolute beginners. In 7 classes go through all the basic steps of bachata, the fundamentals of leading and following and the essential turns of our 6 pattern turn system.

Prerequisites: None

Beginner Level 2

In this series of 7 classes we continue building on what you learned in Level 1. We will continue to add on to the 6 pattern turn system and you will get your first look at 2 of the 5 main bachata positions.

Prerequisites: Beginner 1

Beginner Level 3

In the final level of our beginner series we will go through the 5 main bachata positions, the fundamentals of body movement and isolations, and start with basic ladies styling for the followers.

Prerequisites: Beginner 2

Drop-ins & Socials

You don’t learn to dance by going to classes (as much as we’d like that to be the case). Whilst classes are an important tool to give you the proper technique, the only way to truly learn bachata is by leaving the comfort of the classroom and dancing socially. Therefore we at iDeal Bachata Schools are happy to present you the opportunity to dance socially every monday, wednesday and friday night.

Whilst the party starts at 20:00, the learning starts at 19:00. Our drop-in classes will teach you new and exciting moves that you can use immediately when the music starts, and give you a chance to connect with the partners you will be dancing with. We provide drop-in classes at 3 difficulty levels: beginner (no previous experience with bachata required), improver and medium/advanced.

Intermediate Courses

Intermediate Turn Patterns

Our intermediate turn pattern series of classes takes the 6-pattern turn system (which should be very familiar to you at this point) and takes it up a notch. We're talking double turns, delayed turns and stringing together moves into longer turn patterns.

Prerequisites: Beginner 3

Intermediate Footwork & Solo Shines

What do you do between all the turns and body movements? You take steps. In this course you will learn dozens of different steps you can take with your partner to vary up your dance, as well as get comfortable doing shines (breaking off and dancing solo).

Prerequisites: Beginner 3

Intermediate Body Movement & Positions

Each class in this series takes a deep dive into one of the main body movements or bachata positions, giving you everything you need to make it a beautiful part of your dancing. You will build on what you learned in the beginner series, as well as learn new techniques.

Prerequisites: Beginner 3

In the past we have held workshops teaching Bachata Dominicana, Stage Bachata and many other exciting topics.

Our workshops and bootcamps are the perfect weekend activity, and are always followed by a mini-social just for our participants, if not a full on party open to the public. Do not miss them! Follow us on one of our social media channels or subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to learn when we schedule new workshops.

Advanced Courses

Advanced Turn Combinations

If you like complicated turn patterns then this is the course for you. You will leave with many new high level turn combinations for you to use in your social dancing. The content taught changes after every series conclusion so you can take this course more than once.

Prerequisites: Intermediate Turn Patterns

Advanced Body Movements

The ability to seductively move your own and your partner's body to match the music is what puts the sensual in Bachata Sensual. These classes teach you how to perform and lead the higher difficulty body movements and isolations that will make your dance look sexy as hell.

Prerequisites: Intermediate Body Movement & Positions

Musicality & Choreography

Do you like performing choreography that matches the music perfectly? Then this is the course for you. This course does not teach technique, but you will work together with the teachers to create beautiful choreography that perfectly matches the music of the most popular bachata songs.

Prerequisites: All 3 of our Intermediate courses
